Paper Airplane 101

  • Step 1

    Fold in half lengthwise and then unfold.

  • Step 2

    Fold the top corners in to meet at the center crease.

  • Step 3

    Flip the plane over, and fold the right corner in again to the center crease.

  • Step 4

    Repeat for the left corner.

  • Step 5

    Fold the top point down to meet the bottom of where the previous folds come together.

  • Step 6

    Fold the entire plane in half, in on itself.

  • Step 7

    Fold the wings down so that top fold is parallel with the bottom fold. Repeat on the other side.

  • Step 8

    With the pointed end in front, give a quick flick of the wrist and let go!
